Hiking Blog of Jasmine Zeng

Hello, my name is Jasmine Zeng. I am a hiker, a language enthusiast, a web developer, and a marketing undergrad originally from Zhongshan, Mainland China, currently based in Hong Kong.

While visiting Hong Kong in early 2018, I had a solo hike at the MacLehose Trail Stage 2. I was so enchanted by the natural of this city, which along with other factors led to the thought that relocating there might be a great idea.

I have become a regular hiker after moving to Hong Kong in late 2019. During my treks, I have collected many amazing photos and experiences that I would like to share. Initially, I posted them on Instagram, but because of its limitations, I could only share a few pictures a time with limited words, which brought me to the bold idea of coding my own hiking blog. This was my motivation to web development in late 2021, and I am proud to present this website.
